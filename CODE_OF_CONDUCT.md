# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful and harassment-free community for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience or viewpoint.

## Expected behaviour

Be constructive, assume good faith, respect differing levels of experience, give actionable technical feedback, and keep discussion focused on the work. Harassment, discrimination, threats, deliberate intimidation and disclosure of another person's private information are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and restrict participation when behaviour is inappropriate. Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainers using the contact method configured on the repository host. Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical. This policy applies in repository spaces and when representing the project elsewhere.
